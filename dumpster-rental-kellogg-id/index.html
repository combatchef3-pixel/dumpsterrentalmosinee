Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I get a dumpster delivered?

In most cases, same-day or next-day delivery is available depending on your location and dumpster size.

Can I mix materials in the same dumpster?

Certain restrictions apply. Our team will guide you on separating prohibited items to maintain compliance.

What materials are prohibited?

Hazardous materials like paint, oil, chemicals, and batteries cannot be placed in dumpsters. We can advise on proper disposal alternatives.

Do I need a permit for a dumpster?

Permits may be required if the dumpster sits on public property. We can help with the necessary paperwork.

What happens to the waste after pickup?

Collected waste is transported to certified recycling and processing facilities, where reusable materials are recovered.

Kellogg, ID Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Kellogg is a city in the Silver Valley of Shoshone County, Idaho, United States, in the Idaho Panhandle region. The city lies near the Coeur d'Alene National Forest and about 36 miles (58 km) east-southeast of Coeur d'Alene along Interstate 90. As of the 2010 United States Census, the city had a total population of 2,120, down by a third from its population in 1980.
